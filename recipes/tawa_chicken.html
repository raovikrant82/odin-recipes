Tawa Chicken

Tawa Chicken is a Punjabi recipe where Chicken pieces are marinaded in a paste with Gram Flour and Spices and cooked in a aromatic curry base.

This is a popular street food that is made on large Tawas/a Cast Iron flat or concave round shaped pan usually used for making Rotis/Chapatis. You can of course use a frying pan.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Gram flour
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Spices
    • Turmeric
    • Garam masala
    • Chilli powder
    • Cumin
    • Coriander
    • Fennel
    • Fenugreek seeds
    • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare a paste with Gram flour, Lemon juice, Ginger, Garlic, Turmeric, Coriander, Fennel, Cumin, salt.
  2. Marinate chicken with the paste and leave it for 1 hr.
  3. Cook chicken in oil so that it is evenly fried from all sides and put them aside.
  4. Now prepare curry base with onion and fenugreek fried in oil until they turn brown.
  5. Add ginger and garlic before adding tomatoes.
  6. Ground spices and cook for another 5 mins
  7. Add chicken to the curry base and set stove to low flame. Let it cook for another 5 mins
